About the department

Field research, laboratory training, and academic records in one place.

Established to advance the study of animal biology, the Department of Zoology runs undergraduate and postgraduate programmes covering taxonomy, physiology, ecology, and conservation. This system supports day-to-day academic administration, from batch-wise attendance to semester results and departmental approvals.
